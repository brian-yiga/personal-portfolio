The Sandy's Castle Kindergarten Launch Website is a charming and interactive landing page designed to introduce the school’s mission, values, and enrollment process. Created using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this page features a vibrant, playful design that reflects the welcoming and nurturing environment of the kindergarten.

The site includes engaging visuals, colorful animations, and a user-friendly layout that guides prospective parents and guardians through essential information. A responsive design ensures seamless accessibility across all devices, while the incorporation of call-to-action buttons encourages quick registration and inquiries.

This project showcases my skills in front-end web development, UI/UX design, and creating accessible, mobile-friendly websites, delivering an inviting and informative digital experience for Sandy’s Castle Kindergarten.
